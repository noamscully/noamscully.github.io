Research

I have enjoyed doing research throughout my time at Yale, and I'm very excited to continue in graduate school.

  • The summer before starting college, I did combinatorics research in the 2021 Polymath Jr. Research Experience for Undergraduates with Dr. Eric Rowland. We published our results in the Electronic Journal of Combinatorics, linked here.
  • The following summer, I was awarded the 2022 Yale College First-Year Summer Research Fellowship in the Sciences & Engineering to do astrophysics research with Dr. Daisuke Nagai. I wrote a final report here.
  • In summer 2023, I researched geometry and dynamics as part of Summer Undergraduate Math Research at Yale (SUMRY REU) with Dr. Taylor McAdam and Dr. Fernando Al Assal. Our paper is in preparation.
  • In spring 2024, I did an independent study in category theory with Dr. Pablo Boixeda Alvarez, which concluded with an original research project. You can view the project here.
  • In summer 2024, I was a Visiting Scholar at Boston Univeristy Condensed Matter Theory in Dr. Anatoli Polkovnikov's lab doing research in the Wigner-Weyl correspondence and quantum dynamics.

Teaching

I've enjoyed being an undergraduate learning assistant (ULA) at Yale for proof-based linear algebra and real analysis courses. The job is similar to being a TA, and involves running office hours and small group sessions. I also worked as a private tutor at Yale. Over my gap year before college, I was employed by Art of Problem Solving as a teaching assistant in live virtual classrooms for combinatorics and contest math courses. Finally, in fall 2024, I took a seminar class about teaching math at the college level.

About Me

I am an undergraduate at Yale University in my senior year majoring in mathematics. I'm interested in researching applied math, data science, and theoretical physics. I'm applying to PhD programs in fall 2024.

I have been doing research throughout my time at Yale. The summer before starting college, I did combinatorics research in the 2021 Polymath Jr. Research Experience for Undergraduates with Dr. Eric Rowland. The following summer, I was awarded the 2022 Yale College First-Year Summer Research Fellowship in the Sciences & Engineering to do astrophysics research with Dr. Daisuke Nagai. In summer 2023, I researched geometry and dynamics as part of Summer Undergraduate Math Research at Yale (SUMRY REU) with Dr. Taylor McAdam and Dr. Fernando Al Assal. In spring 2024, I did an independent study in category theory with Dr. Pablo Boixeda Alvarez, which concluded with an original research project. Finally, in summer 2024, I was a Visiting Scholar at Boston Univeristy Condensed Matter Theory in Dr. Anatoli Polkovnikov's lab.
